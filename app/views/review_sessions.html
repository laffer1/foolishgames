Sessions @ AOL, A review

The Sessions @ AOL CD is a good buy for Jewel fans. It includes a live version of "Intuition" and the video performance. You can play the video on Mac OS X, Mac OS 9 and Windows systems. The CD includes a Macromedia presentation to load the video, although you can just access it directly as jewel.mov. (QuickTime Format) I think the Jewel content alone makes it worth the price of admission.

In addition to Jewel content, the CD includes tracks from Coldplay, Missy Elliot, Avril Lavigne, Michelle Branch, and several more. It is a good CD for guests because of the variety of artists.
