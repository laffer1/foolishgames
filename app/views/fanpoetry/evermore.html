Evermore

 
   Shattered 
and ringing in her ears. 
The icicles 
hit 
a perfect C. 
Disjointing 
her orientation. 
Left at the mercy of Sorrow. 
She speaks aloud 
to the darkness. 
And the stars whisper 
among themselves. 
Crying aloud 
to the darkness. 
Her lips motion, 
witout a sound. 
Her head, 
damp with all natural wetness. 
Sweat, tears 
and Joy. 
Her mind grows weary, 
but her soul is yearning, 
Evermore.
