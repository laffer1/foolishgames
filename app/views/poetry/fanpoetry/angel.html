Scarlet Angel

 
I never meant to betray myself 
As this vessel of darkness - I’ve learned too much 
There is a true abscence within - us all 
Goddess inhabits all souls 
I will not enjoy this battle - contrary to popular society 
I want to help - no longer can 
For I was taught to not confess, I never cared 
But for this moment, I love 
Give me hope, given peace 
Share all in this river of scarlet 
Final pleas nurture me 
As you feel fear - safety is my pure arms 
No mother loved you like I do 
My love is greater 
Your first and last true kiss comes from me 
Your will is mine 
I control while you order 
Forever our passion will be romanticized 
Every word I speak - is from you 
All you fear comes from me 
I occupy you 
Remember I serve you 
Don’t be surprised - I’ve always loved you 
Come to me - for I’m your scarlet angel.
