Guidelines:

  1. Must be suitable for general audiences.
  2. Can be in the following languages: English, German and French
  3. Must include a name and proper email address when you submit.  (ie.  yourusername@yourisp.com, screenname@aol.com)
  4. Please use the spacing and punctuation that you desire, it will be published the way you submit it to me.

If you submit anything I assume you agreed to the following statements:

  1. You understand that your work will first be reviewed for content by either the webmaster for FG or myself.
  2. I can remove your poetry at anytime.
  3. Any of your work will not be passed on to other publications (web or print) by myself without your written permission.
