I dream too....

 
  Every night I sit and stare, 
At the beautiful moonlight glare. 

I dream about you and me, 
how happy we could be. 

If we never let reality take place, 
I guess I'll just cut to the chase. 

I wish it was still you and me, 
but I guess all I can do is dream.
