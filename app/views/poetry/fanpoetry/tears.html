Unwept Tears

 
"From ashes to ashes.." was heard 
Amid the weeping of those who valued their strength 
Under her calm facade, she felt nothing 
But unwept tears 
Like the swelling of rain 
Within stormy clouds 
Before that fatal moment 
During her life was only time 
Beyond the rain and storms and death 
Among the passing of forgotten dreams
