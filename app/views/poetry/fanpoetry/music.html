Music To My Ears

 

The music you
play fills the
air so sweetly.
You play your
guitar as if
it was an
old friend you
were having a
conversation with.
Your voice so
rugged yet so
smooth calms
my troubled soul.
How I wish
the whole world
could here you
o they can
feel the way
I do when
you play.
