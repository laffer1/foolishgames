Green

 

A world which bleeds,
the people who stab it,
the past, the story
the present, the path
the future, the result

Children, the dream
hope,the tool for the dreams
adults, the represers
society, the rutine

Books,the wisdom
minds,the treasures
music, the expression
writting, the revalation

You, the judge
life, the chance
determination, the strength
honesty , the essence

Fight, belive, love, and be true to yourself.....
