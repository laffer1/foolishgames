Garden Of Love

       Walk with me my child,
              Through my endless garden of love,
          The garden which I must pray for everyday,
           If my garden wilts...then I shall pass,
       Needless to say, I wouldn't want to wilt as do my roses,
              Say you will pray for my garden too,
       The love I shall miss of my eternal daffodils, 
     My life is a simple one...simple like the flowers,
      Yet still as mortal.
