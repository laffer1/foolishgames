May I Have This Dance 
I want to dance with you deep into night 
Being with you just feels way to right 
If you left right now, my whole world would end 
A cure of love your sweet voice does send 
I long just to touch you and you to touch me 
Deep in your arms is where I have to be 
How can I know if you  even care? 
How can I know if we'd be a good pair? 
Well,God will finish this soon, but how? 
I don't know any answers at least not for now 
So I hope to ask you if even by chance 
Could I possibly have this last dance?
