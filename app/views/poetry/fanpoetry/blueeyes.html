Blue Eyes

My heart fell victum to the force of your gravity.
Golden burning cinders swept away by the sea in your eyes.
The pleas of the voices in me saturate your closed ears.
Turning your face away.
The full hallowness of your breath caresses my lonely skin.
My dry tears leak onto you.
You remain unnoticing, even for your distance.
misunderstood, I cried.
