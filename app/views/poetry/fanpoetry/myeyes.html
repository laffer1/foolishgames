MY EYES NOW SEE

 I was always on the outside
Looking in
You could hear me,
You could see me,
But I was ignored.

Once again I'm on the outside,
But I'm no longer looking in.
I am roaming free,
Around the lawn, the garden
And the flowers listen
Intently

You're once again drowning,
But it is no longer I 
Who must save you
And at first that was hurtful,
But now it's just
Convienient
