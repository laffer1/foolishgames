Is it today, 
Or is it tomorrow, 
But as the waves roll on, 
I realise, 
Tomorrow never comes. 
Today has gone, 
And my tears are still strong, 
But I'm still here, 
And I have nothing to go on. 
Help me please, 
Tell me it's OK. 
Re-write my faith, 
Tomorrow's another day
