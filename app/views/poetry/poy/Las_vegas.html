Pieces of You

Las Vegas and Leaving Las Vegas

Women who suck
their cigarettes
as though they were
giving their
hatred head
Bill, Butch, and Bart
Swapping penis size
in the front seat
while Thelma, Theisel and
Lou Lou up there
bouffant hairdos
and secretely go
where Blue eyeshadow
has never gone before
