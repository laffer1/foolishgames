High School Pictures

Click on an image to see a larger version.

These photographs were taken at Interlochen Arts Academy in Interlochen, MI.

The third image was taken in the Fine Arts Building. It was also suggested that it was taken during a Coffee House performance. Coffee House performances happen once a month during the school year, and feature students who perform in the order in which they sign up. No one but the person performing is aware of the content. Typically staff members do not participate, so practically anything can be performed without fear of disciplinary action. Thanks to Erinn for this information.
