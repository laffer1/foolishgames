Love is blind
-said the voice of the wind
so I closed my eyes
and waited for time
to melt the ice

but winter
only scattered the
rainbow's light

the Moon and the Pleiades
are set
Midnight
and Time spins away

beneath my feet
the earth so warm

a twig to be thrown
against the splashing 
waves- soon sucked
under to sink again

Wasted Time
